Sean's Van

Hyper-Mobile Tiny Home AKA Super-Decked-out Campervan. 84 sq. ft. of solar and compost-powered living on wheels.

Solar · Refrigerator · Gas Stove · Sink · Hot Water · Furnace · Composting Toilet · Household Electrical · Couch & Bed · Standing Desk · Rooftop · Motorcycle Carrier

The home is built on top of a 2004 Ford E350 Super Duty Extended chassis. The original living conversion was done by Sportsmobile, with many upgrades done by Sean.
